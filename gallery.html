Objective 3

Create visuals that exhibit professional quality in terms of camerawork, composition, and lighting.

Conceptual Shooting

This was assignment 3.1 from DVA101, where we were tasked with conveying a message through only visuals. No sound was allowed, and we were only supposed to rely on lighting, composition, and camerawork. This meets Objective Three because I was forced to focus on only camerawork, composition, and lighting.

Anti-Littering PSA

This was the final assignment for DVA101, where we were to create a 30-second advertisement or a 30-second Public Service Announcement. This meets Objective Three because I focused on good composition, following the rule of thirds. I also had to ensure the lighting remained soft because I was shooting in daylight, so I used shadows to my advantage.

Ship Over Earth

This is a personal exercise I did using Nuke. This meets Objective Four because I composited all these digital assets, including Earth, Earth lights, Earth atmosphere, Earth clouds, a ship, stars, and a lens flare, into one scene using industry-standard software and techniques.

Title Sequence

This was my final assignment for DVA254. The task was to create a minimum 30-second-long title sequence for a made-up film. This meets Objective Four because all assets were animated and composited together in After Effects.
